Work at Home Mom

A way to work inside your comfort zone... literally!

A work at home parent is someone who conducts remote work from home and integrates parenting into his or her working time and workspace. They are sometimes referred to as a WAHM (work at home mom) or a WAHD (work at home dad). Parents who work from home can customize their day to meet their family's needs. More family time and no commute? Sounds great!
